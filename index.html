The language they developed was initially named "Oak" after an oak tree that stood outside Gosling's office. Pretty solid, right? üå≥ They also considered names like "Silk." But, as fate (and trademark law) would have it, "Oak" was already taken.

Legend has it the team brainstormed new names at a local coffee shop, and voila! Inspired by the Peet's Coffee they were enjoying, the name "Java" was born! ‚òï Eureka!
